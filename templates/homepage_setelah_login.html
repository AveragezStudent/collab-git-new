About Us

History of LATEN GYM

Laten Gym was founded in 2019 and has been operating for four years. In a relatively short time, Latent Gym has succeeded in attracting hundreds of members to join.

Unlike the gyms in general, Laten Gym is an independent gym that does not provide personal trainers. Even so, members can still achieve their own fitness and health goals by using the modern and complete facilities available in the gym.

Since its inception, Laten Gym has been committed to providing the best gym experience and making its members feel satisfied with the services provided. This gym offers a supportive environment for anyone looking to achieve their fitness and health goals.
